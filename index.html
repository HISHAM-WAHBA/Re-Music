About

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    RE Music is Egypt's premium music production and distribution organisation. RE Music manages and controls several labels that target different genres of Arabic and International music, labels such as Nagham Zone, Moseeqa Network, Pyramedia, Awtar, ElKhan, ElWaha, Relax Arabia, DJ Sha3by and more. Furthermore, RE Music manages content production & development for major Arabic artists like Mostafa Shawky, Tamer karawan, Shaimaa Elshayb, Fatma Eid, Mounir & Sherif Wesseimy, Abdel Rahman Balala, Majd Elkassem and many others. RE Music owns a large library of Arabic music reaching up to 15000 tracks covering golden oldies like Amr Diab, Angham, Omar Khairat, Aly Haggar, Mohamed ELhelw, Medhat Saleh, in addition to major Pop artists like Masar Egbary, Loai, Hossam Habib, Ehab Tawfick and others. RE Music focuses on developing new emerging artists like Angham Abdeen, Mahmoud Akram and others. The company was setup by Richard Elhaj a well known producer in the Arab World who has operated in the Middle East since 2004. The company is well connected with all major music platforms and works in partnership with Youtube, TikTok and Believe Digital and is constantly developing strong alliances and networks.
